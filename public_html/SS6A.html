Special Session on Recent Progress in Operator Algebras (Code: SS 6A)

General Information about the Meeting

This special session is part of the 2011 Fall Central Section Meeting of the American Mathematical Society. The meeting will be held October 14-16, 2011 (Friday - Sunday) at University of Nebraska-Lincoln, Lincoln, NE. Here is a tentative schedule for the meeting.

For additional information, there is also a local webpage including a local information page describing Information for Presenters, Computing Facilities, the Book Sale, and so on.
