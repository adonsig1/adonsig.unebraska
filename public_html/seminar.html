Jan 17 Ken Davidson (of the University of Waterloo) Atomic Representations of 2-graph algebras
Abstract: The (nonself-adjoint) operator algebra associated to a 2-graph on a single vertex is just an algebra generated by a finite number of generators with special relations and a natural norm. An atomic representation has an orthonormal basis on which the generators act as partial isometries that permute the basis up to scalar multiples. I will explain how these arise, and how to classify the maximal completely contractive ones.

This is joint work with Stephen Power and Dilian Yang.
