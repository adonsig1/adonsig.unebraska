Quizzes

Here is a list of all the quiz questions. Since the answers are clearly stated in the lectures and the textbook, I don't give them here.

1 Define 'f : R to R is continuous at a in R'.
2 For S contained in Rn, define 1) 'S is open' and 2) 'a in Rn is a boundary point of S'.
3 State the 'Limits and Components' Theorem.
4 Suppose D, contained in Rn is open and f : D to Rm is a function. Define 'f is differentiable at a in D' and the differential dfa.
5 State the 'Differentiability' Theorem.
6 State the Chain Rule.
7 State the theorem on 'Mixed Partials'.
8 Suppose S contained in Rn is open and f from S to R. Define the Taylor polynomial of order k for f at a in S.
9 State Taylor's theorem, for a function f from S to R, where S contained in Rn is open.
10 Suppose S contained in Rn is open and a is in S. Define `f from S to Rn is locally invertible around a'.
11 State the Inverse Function Theorem.
12 Suppose a,x in Rm, b,y in Rn, G is a function from Rm to Rn, and G(a,b)=0. Define a local solution of G(x,y)=0 for y in terms of x around (a,b).
13 If M is a subset of Rn and p is in M, define Tp M, the set of tangent vectors to M at p.
