\question{
Let $f : \bR^n \to \bR$ be differentiable on $\bR^n$ and $f(\bx) = 0$ for all $\bx \in \bR^n$ with
$\|\bx\|=1$. 
Show that there exists a point $\bc \in B_1(\zero)$ such that $df_\bc$ is the zero transformation.
(This is a version of Rolle's Theorem in $\bR^n$.)

You may assume that $\overline{B_1(\zero)}$ is compact.
}{
